A site to create cryptographic hashes of jokes to prove authorship. Created for a hackathon, from idea to published with PR video in 6 hours.

### Video Splitter
A program to automatically edit videos based on scripts and audio content using forced alignment.

### Correct App
A grammar correction app that uses NLP to find common English mistakes made by native speakers of Japanese. Poster [Research Poster(PDF)](files/Correct_Poster.pdf)

### [Not My Audience](https://notmyaudience.com/)
A live interactive presentation where audience members vote on their own devices to influence a comedy show.

### Podcast Practice App
A service that provides podcasts and scripts synced together as a study aid.

### [Teaching Tools](https://teachingtools.herokuapp.com/)
A tool to automatically generate teaching worksheets and output them to printable PDFS.
